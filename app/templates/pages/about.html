What is jCourse for?


jCourse is built to aid Jacobs University students choose courses by offering them acces to information that would otherwise be attained in a less comfortable way. jCourse uses data collected from CampusNet of Jacobs University Bremen. The data is collected from the course catalogue of academic year 2014 (this data is all public). Through jCourse we aim to offer a more convenient and accessible way of rating, reviewing and comparing courses.

jCourse at ths stage is meant to help students choose their courses and help each-other. This is just the testing phase of a platform with a wider set of functionalities at the core of which lies the facilitation of academic interaction
